Admin PIN (login to admin.html)
  • Default: 2026  (if you never changed it)
  • Change inside: open admin.html → log in → tab "Admin PIN"
  • Or edit in code: script.js → FC.pins.admin = "yourpin"

Replace your logo file:
  Put your PNG/JPG in assets/ and in Admin → Website set "Logo image URL" to e.g. ./assets/my-logo.png
  Or upload the logo in Admin (saves in the browser).
